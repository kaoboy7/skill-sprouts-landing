Here's something you can try right now

Based on what you shared, here are simple scripts for real moments.

Tantrums

When your child is melting down…

Don't say

"Stop crying right now."

Say this instead

"I see you're upset. It's okay to have big feelings. I'm right here with you."

Why it works

It calms the moment instead of escalating it.

Not Listening

When your child ignores you…

Don't say

"How many times do I have to tell you?!"

Say this instead

"Shoes on, please. Do you want to walk or hop to the door?"

Why it works

It gives a simple direction and a small choice—so your child feels in control while still doing what you asked.
